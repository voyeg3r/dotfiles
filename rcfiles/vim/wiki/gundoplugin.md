### instalação

Para quem usa o PathogenPlugin

hg clone http://bitbucket.org/sjl/gundo.vim ~/.vim/bundle/gundo

Add a mapping to your ~/.vimrc (change the key to suit your taste):

nnoremap <F5> :GundoToggle<CR>
